Hello, I'm Shreyash Murkar

I am a dedicated and passionate artist who finds inspiration in the world around me. Through my work, I explore emotions, light, and color to tell stories that connect with the viewer on a deeper level. Each piece I create reflects a journey — a moment of thought, feeling, or imagination brought to life on canvas or digital medium. I strive to evoke emotion, spark curiosity, and inspire others through my art. Whether it’s capturing the serenity of nature, the mystery of night, or the vibrancy of human expression, my goal is to create works that resonate and leave a lasting impression.

I’m an artist inspired by the calm and mystery of the night. My works often feature deep contrasts and rich shadows, representing emotions that shine brightest in the dark.
